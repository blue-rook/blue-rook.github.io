Blue Rook Technology is a nimble business driven by a group of engineers from different backgrounds that are all about Data and AI solutions. We deliver optimal solutions for a diverse range of clients—from small health care centers to Brazil’s largest infrastructure players.

Our Home Base: Florianópolis - SC

We call Florianópolis, SC our home—a place officially hailed as the “Capital da Startup”. Nestled on an island brimming with natural beauty and innovative potential, this strategic location has powered our journey since 2022, serving as a vibrant hub to connect with the best business partners and tech talent.

Reach out and talk with real people instead of generative AI—for a refreshing, human conversation!

Gralha

Deep within Brazil’s lush landscapes, a striking avian marvel soars through the skies—the Blue Rook, locally revered as the Gralha Azul. With its brilliant blue plumage and inquisitive gaze, this bird represents the spirit and untamed beauty of its homeland.

The Blue Rook thrives across a mosaic of habitats—from the dense, verdant rainforests to urban oases nestled in bustling cities.
